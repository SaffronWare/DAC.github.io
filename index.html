About DAC

The DAC (Dawson Aerospace Club), founded in 2026, aims to propel dawson students into the field of aerospace. We hope to unite students from different programs under interdisciplinary aerospace projects.

At DAC, we hope to sharpen our skills in various fields, whether it be engineering, physics, computer science, or design.

Our Mission

  • Promote aerospace research at Dawson College
  • Give a boost to aspiring student's careers
  • Build a community revolving around aerospace
  • Build projects in a team
